Justin Wang

My name is Justin Wang and I have always desired to enter the Computer Science engineering field. I have only just recently started to learn about computer programming and I know that I still have much to learn. However, my main focus is to improve and learn as much as possible whether it be the simple or complex. I will pay close attention to anything that is placed in front of me and I am willing to work with anyone despite our differences in opinion. I will keep an open mind and a clear head only focusing on the task at hand.
I have always found an interest in computer science and I know that it is not only based around computers. Programming is a language that allows us to communicate with all types of technology. Simply turning on a cell phone requires a bit of code. Everything works as a stimulus and a response and programming is the bridge that connects them. Understanding how things respond and communicate with one another is an experience on its own. Initiating a response is a gift that should not be taken for granted. I plan to use programming to create the software of the future.
